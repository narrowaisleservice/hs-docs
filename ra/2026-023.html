Exposure to chemicals Ill health, burns, loss of consciousness A 4 3 12

  • Booth minimises paint in workshop

  • Air gun soaked in thinners overnight when no workers present

  • Thinners kept with lids on when not in use

  • COSHH assessments in place for all chemicals used

  • Air monitoring assessment in place

  • Air fed masks provided to spray operatives

  • Eye protection provided to workers

  • Protective gloves provided to workers

  • Specific storage facilities provided for paint

  • Health surveillance provided to workers

  • Workers provided with suitable & sufficient training in relation to booth operation

  • Signage installed at spray booth

 4 2 8 M

  • Inspect and manage use of PPE throughout workforce
