Falls from Height Death, broken bones, fractures etc. E 5 3 15

  • NO leaning from the basket or climbing on handrails etc.

  • Work is only undertaken from the basket deck.

  • MEWP never moved whilst operatives are elevated.

  • MEWP is only used by operators with a current full IPAF Licence (Correct category).

  • MEWP is serviced and has 6 monthly Thorough Examination certificate.

  • Pre use checks to ensure there are no overhead hazards (Gantry’s, cables, services, overhangs etc.)

  • Max Safe Working Load must be adhered to.

  • Toolbox talks on working from height delivered.

 5 2 10 M

  • Document and communicate a rescue plan.

  • Ensure MEWP is subject to a visual inspection prior to use.
Falling Objects Head injuries, cuts etc A 3 3 9

  • Ensure that no objects are stored on basket deck

  • Exclusion zone created with plastic barriers in public areas around MEWP and working area.

  • Make other employees/contractors aware of your work.

  • Do not lean from basket.

  • Marshal is used on the ground to stop interference wearing a hard hat.

 3 1 3 ✓ Acceptable

  • Carry out a daily pre-use checks of MEWP.
MEWP tipping over Death, crushing A 5 3 15

  • MEWP is only used on solid, flat stable ground.

  • MEWP is not overloaded and the SWL is not exceeded.

  • DO NOT drive over pot holes, manholes, kerbs, slopes or anything that may destabilise the MEWP.

  • Max Safe Working Load must be adhered to.

  • MEWP must never be placed over forks when working at front of truck. Forks must be removed prior to work commencing. Refer to SWP 011

 5 1 5 ✓ Acceptable

  • Carry out a daily pre-use checks of MEWP.
Contact with overhead hazards Death, broken bones, fractures etc. E 4 3 12

  • Do not use within 7m of electricity cables.

  • Do not move the MEWP until you are certain there are no overhead hazards where you are working.

  • ALWAYS lower MEWP before moving position.

  • Pre use checks to ensure there are no overhead hazards (Gantry’s, cables, services, overhangs etc.)

 4 1 4 ✓ Acceptable

  • Carry out a daily pre-use checks of MEWP.
MEWP being struck by vehicle Death, broken bones, fractures etc. E 4 2 8

  • Any MEWP work is undertaken in designated areas away from other vehicle movements (robots/FLT’s)

  • Exclusion zone created with barriers around MEWP when working close to traffic routes.

  • Signs are posted to warn of MEWP in use.

 4 1 4 ✓ Acceptable

  • Carry out a daily pre-use checks of MEWP.
Crushing underneath the basket Death, broken bones A 5 2 10

  • Guards are safety devices are in place and fitted.

  • Operator checks that there is nobody underneath the basket before lowering it.

 5 1 5 ✓ Acceptable

  • Carry out a daily pre-use checks of MEWP.
