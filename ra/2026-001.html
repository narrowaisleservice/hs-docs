Fatalities		 A 5 4 20

  • High visibility jackets must be worn in the yard

  • Only authorised persons permitted in yard

  • Safety footwear must be worn when entering warehouse and factory.

  • 10mph speed limit in the yard and car parking areas.

  • Drivers to give right of way to pedestrians

  • Mandatory signage displayed must be observed by pedestrians and drivers at all times.

  • The roller shutter doors to the buildings must not be used for pedestrian access and egress.

  • Only authorised, licensed and insured drivers should drive company vehicles, including forklift trucks.

  • FLT Drivers complete daily safety checks.

  • Pedestrians must follow marked walkways throughout the site

  • A pedestrian crossing/ zebra crossing area, and road markings in place to give priority to pedestrians who move between offices and workshop
