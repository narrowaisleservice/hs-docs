Falls From Height (ladders and steps) Death, broken bones, fractures etc. E 4 3 12

  • ALWAYS look to work from the ground where reasonably practical to do so.

  • Use working platform or pop up where reasonably practical to do so.

  • Ladders/steps are visually inspected prior to use.

  • Do not use defective ladders.

  • Employees are trained and competent in use of equipment.

  • Employees wear suitable safety footwear.

  • Ladder is clean and free from oil/grease etc.

 4 1 4 ✓ Acceptable

  • Only use a ladder / steps where all other safer alternatives have been considered first.

  • Ensure ladders are only used for light work and short periods. (30 minutes/10kg guide)

  • Ensure three points of contact are always made with the ladder.

  • Ensure the ladder is of suitable size and construction.
Falling Objects Head injuries, cuts etc A 3 3 9

  • No objects are stored on stepladder platforms.

  • Exclusion zone created with cones and physical barriers.

  • Employees/contractors made aware of work.

  • Access equipment is not left unattended.

 3 1 3 ✓ Acceptable

  • Monitor and Maintain existing controls
Defective Ladder Death, broken bones, fractures etc. E 4 3 12

  • Ladder is logged and regularly inspected for defects.

 4 2 8 M

  • Ensure ladders and steps are stored appropriately.

  • Ensure that damaged ladders are destroyed and suitably disposed of.
Ladder being struck by plant or vehicles Death, broken bones, fractures etc. E 4 2 8

  • Ladder work is undertaken in designated areas away from vehicle and plant movements.

  • All operatives are made aware when ladder work is being undertaken.

  • Walkways are clearly marked on the floors of the factory.

 4 1 4 ✓ Acceptable

  • Monitor and Maintain existing controls
Ladder Slipping Death, broken bones, fractures etc. E 4 3 12

  • Ladder is only used on dry, flat surfaces.

  • Rubber feet are properly fitted and maintained in good condition.

  • Ladder is footed and tied off.

  • Moveable steps used wherever possible.

  • Workers never lean on ladder.

  • Workers never carry equipment or materials up ladder.

 4 1 4 ✓ Acceptable

  • Monitor and Maintain existing controls
