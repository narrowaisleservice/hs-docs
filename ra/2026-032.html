Fire and Explosion Damage to building and multiple injuries / fatal accidents A 5 2 10

  • Equipment is fit for purpose and well maintained.

  • Area is physically protected with screens or solid barriers to protect passers-by.

  • Battery disconnected from forklift before welding begins

  • Forklift stripped of flammable parts, as far as possible.

  • Good housekeeping standards maintained and flammable materials are removed from welding area.

  • Welding equipment is fitted with flashback arrestors etc.

  • Pipe work and connections are regularly visually checked

  • Powder fire extinguisher is close to hand.

  • Hot work never left unattended –items have been allowed to cool prior to leaving the site.

  • Welders are trained and competent.

  • Welding in an environment where flammable atmosphere exists from paint fumes or charging batteries for example is never undertaken.

  • Process explained to site contact and best place to carry out work identified.

  • Truck moved outside, wherever possible.

  • We operate within the customer Permit to Work system, as required.

  • Workers aware of nearest Fire Exit and the Assembly Point for the site.

 5 1 5 ✓ Acceptable

  • Maintain existing controls
Burns and scalds and exposure to UV Burns, scalds etc. form hot surfaces E 4 2 8

  • Welding undertaken by trained and competent persons only.

  • Full PPE required for welding – suitably graded welding mask, leather gauntlets and apron / proban overalls.

  • all skin is covered – proban overalls

  • Area is physically protected with welding curtains or physical barrier

  • Welders are trained and competent.

 4 1 4 ✓ Acceptable

  • Display Signage near to work area warning off the risk.
Manual handling equipment and gas bottles Back injuries, sprains, strains etc E 4 3 12

  • Vehicle parked as close as possible to work area

  • Truck moved to work area before removing power supply

  • employees are trained in manual handling techniques.

 4 1 4 ✓ Acceptable
Arc eye / flash – operator and passers by Eye injuries and potential permanent damage A 3 3 9

  • Suitably graded welding mask always used

  • Area is physically protected with welding curtains or solid barrier.

 3 1 3 ✓ Acceptable

Exposure to welding fume Lung disease, irritation E 4 3 12
