Control Measures — Restrictions — Special Instructions
  • YP has had a full site induction by Manager
  • YP to be supervised at all times by a senior operative
  • YP to be taken on full tour of premises and instructed on no-go areas (Fork truck area, factory, warehouse)
  • Max. limit of 10kgs to be set for lifting, if within persons capability
  • YP to be instructed in manual handling techniques prior to starting
  • YP to be supervised at all times
  • YP to be given regular breaks and permitted working hours must not be exceeded
  • YP to be issued with appropriate PPE (e.g. safety shoes and gloves etc)
  • YP to be instructed not to use or approach fork lift truck
  • Extra vigilance from existing staff to ensure YP is supervised
  • YP to be fully supervised and instructed not to use other tools or equipment
  • Supervisor to enforce hazardous areas for YP
  • YP is permitted to work with hazardous substances but under supervision
  • Ensure Manager makes YP aware of substances used on site and the hazards they present
  • Make Safety data sheets available to YP
  • YP not permitted to use vibrating tools or equipment
  • YP to raise concerns directly with Responsible Manager
  • Regular meetings with Responsible Manager
  • Ensure ongoing training in safe use of welding equipment, angle grinders, abrasive wheels, circular saws, orbital sanders and various hand operated power tools
