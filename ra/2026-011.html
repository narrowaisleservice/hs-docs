Cutting by exposed part Cut injury E 4 2 8

  • Exposed rotating parts are minimised, by design

  • Adjusting guard covers as much of the blade as possible, only opening on downward pressure

  • Control requires operating hand to be away from moving part

  • Power is isolated before changing blade

  • Blade is braked and stops within 10 seconds

  • Guard is checked prior to each use
