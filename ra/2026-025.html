Forklift Truck rollover/Chain breaking Crushing injuries
Plant/ Equipment damage
Death		 A 5 3 15

  • Only trained and competent personnel authorised to operate the tilt table.

  • Pre use checks of the chains and hydraulic equipment on the tilt table.

  • Load is securely attached to forks and will not shift or fall during operations. Forklift forks inserted into the test weight pockets under the load, and ratchet strap attached the load onto the back of the forks.

  • Area is cordoned off depending on the height of the mast, to mark a safe distance which will keep people out of the way in the event of a truck rollover.

  • Workers trained to be prepared to deal with a complete rollover of the forklift truck in the event that it should happen.

  • Relevant workers informed in advance when tilt test will take place should any test weights be required in the production line.

  • During tilt table operation, no persons are allowed to stand or work within the operating radius, e.g. loading area or the compressor house. Stand well clear
