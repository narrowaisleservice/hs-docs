Hazard Effect At Risk Sev. Prob. Rating Existing Control Measures Sev. Prob. Rating Accept? Additional Control Measures Done
High pressure steam Burns or scalds E 3 3 9

  • Only trained and authorised operatives are permitted to operate the steam cleaner.

  • Protective gear i.e. overalls or waterproof jacket, and gloves worn by workers.

  • Full face shield /safety goggles worn by workers.

  • Water supply is turned on before running the steam cleaner.

  • Pre use checks of hoses undertaken.

  • Weather conditions taken into consideration prior to use (High winds).

 3 1 3 ✓ Acceptable

  • Monitor and maintain existing controls.
Flying debris Eye and face injuries E 3 3 9

  • Safety glasses/ visors worn to prevent flying debris from entering eyes.

  • Steam cleaning area checked before switching on steam cleaner to lessen potential of debris being lifted up and blown around by steam cleaner.

 3 1 3 ✓ Acceptable

  • Monitor and maintain existing controls.
Kerosene /fumes Kerosene spillage Choking/ respiratory system irritation E 3 3 9

  • Doors to compressor house where steam cleaner is located are kept open during cleaning operations.

  • Steam cleaner is serviced and maintained.

  • Follow training and instructions given in the use of steam cleaning equipment.

  • Dispense the paraffin using a funnel to avoid spillage.

  • Wash hands before eating or smoking to remove kerosene/ paraffin smell.

 3 1 3 ✓ Acceptable

  • Monitor and maintain existing controls.
Fire Fire/ explosion E 4 4 12

  • Kerosene/ paraffin kept in locked Paint Store, by entrance to compressor house.

  • Fuel tank filled when machine is cold

 4 1 4 ✓ Acceptable

  • Monitor and maintain existing controls.
Slips, trips and falls Various injuries A 3 3 9

  • Fuel tank filled when machine is cold

  • All steam cleaning operations are undertaken from the ground wherever practicable.

  • Suspend steam cleaning operations suspended in adverse weather conditions (if ground is slippery, frosty or icy).

  • Appropriate safety shoes or wellingtons worn.

 3 1 3 ✓ Acceptable

  • Monitor and maintain existing controls.
