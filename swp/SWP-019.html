📝 Sequence of Work
Setting Procedures for Flexi Brakes
1
Refer to SWP 001 Before Starting Work/ Putting Forklift Truck in Safe Position before carrying out the following work.
2
If battery is in the truck disconnect it. If the truck is masted, ensure battery is in the truck before jacking it up.
3
Chock the rear wheel opposite to the one being removed.
4
Place a toe jack under the side counter weight just behind the wheel to be removed. Ensure it is on the right jacking point i.e. flat surface behind rear wheel arch, Picture 1.
5
N.B. Check jacking point for damage.
6
Raise the wheel so that the wheel is just clear off the floor to allow rotation (max 15mm) and lower the chassis onto the FLT block system (Resin blocks) under the counter weight section, Picture 2.
