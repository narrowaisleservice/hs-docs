📝 Sequence of Work
Raising Forklift truck off the ground
1
Refer to SWP 001 Before Starting Work/ Putting Forklift Truck in Safe Position before carrying out the following work.
2
Before any jacking up of truck, ensure battery is in place for stability.
3
(IF THE BATTERY HAS TO REMOVED FOR BRAKE BLEEDING PURPOSES, STEERING MUST BE STRAIGHT AND DO NOT JACK ABOVE 3 CHOCKING BLOCKS HIGH)
4
Unplug battery from truck.
5
Chock the wheel opposite to the one being raised (on the same axle) See picture 1.
6
Place a toe jack under the side counterweight just behind the wheel arch to be removed. Ensure it is on the right jacking point. (See Picture 2.)
7
Picture 1 Picture 2
N.B. Check jacking point for damage prior to jacking.
8
Raise the wheel so that the wheel is just clear off the floor approx 10mm and ensure that all component parts of the FLT Block System are free of dirt and contamination. Ensure that the Base Block is always used at the bottom. Lower the chassis onto the FLT block system as shown in Picture 3 (rear) and Picture 4 (front).
9
Picture 3 (rear) Picture 4 (Front)
N.B. Always use the block with the serrated surface as the top block in any stack
If the incorrect Block is used as the base,
then the system could fail. ALWAYS USE THE
BASE BLOCK AT THE BOTTOM.
(Largest surface area)
DO NOT EXCEED 5 BLOCKS IN A STACK
