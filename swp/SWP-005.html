📝 Sequence of Work
Drive Motor Removal and Replacement
1
Refer to SWP 001 Before Starting Work/ Putting Forklift Truck in Safe Position before carrying out the following work (it is important to disconnect the battery). The mast and forks should be fully lowered to the ground (if they are still attached on the truck).
2
For ease of drive motor removal, turn steering to the full right hand lock, Picture 1.
3
Note: The wheel-shaped chocks on which the wheels are resting help to raise the forklift slightly from the ground so that the special motor support jack/ drive motor trolley can fit in under the truck and you can gain access to the drive motor.
4
Ensure handbrake is applied, and rear wheels are chocked to prevent movements.
5
Isolate the battery by releasing the battery plug sited on the right hand side of the panel below the hydraulic lever controls.
6
Remove the M20 cap head setscrew retaining the door counter weight.
7
Fully open the door counter weight to gain access to the drive motor and cables and chock open with a wedge block; (check hinge weld/ pin condition before opening counter weight, and be cautious of the counterweight door dropping to the ground).
8
Disconnect the drive motor cables and auxiliary plug and pull clear of the motor.
9
Place the drive motor trolley/ special motor support jack in position and only take the weight of the motor, Picture 2.
10
Remove the eight M10 setscrews from around the motor – gearbox, Picture 3.
11
Using a suitable lever crowbar move the motor away horizontally from the axle until the spline shaft is clear of the axle and axle spigot, Picture 4.
