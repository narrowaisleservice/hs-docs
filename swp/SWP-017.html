📝 Sequence of Work
Removal and Refitting the Seat Assembly
1
Refer to SWP 001 Before Starting Work/ Putting Forklift Truck in Safe Position before carrying out the following work.
2
Disconnect the battery, remove the plug and lead through the opening at the rear of the seat pan.
3
Remove the seat belt actuator from the left hand side of the seat.
4
Remove M8 cap head setscrew from the seat frame.
5
Remove seat by sliding fully forward off the runners.
6
Disconnect wiring from seat and light switch. Move clear of the seat frame assembly.
7
Disconnect the gas strut by removing the ball joint retaining clip at the end fastened to the chassis.
8
Remove two M10 hex head set bolts securing the seat frame to the chassis. Lift the seat frame clear of the truck.
9
Refitting the seat assembly is the reversal of the above.
10
Caution: The seat assembly is heavy and awkward to manoeuvre, seek assistance when removing from the truck.
