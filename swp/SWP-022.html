📝 Sequence of Work
Storing Flexi Trucks in Shipping Containers
Note: Only qualified/competent operators who have attended a Flexi Truck licence should carry out the following procedure.
1
Ensure the shipping containers doors are secured fully in the open position before driving in or out of containers. (See Picture 1)
2
Drive the Flexi Truck forwards into the container using the vehicle lights to illuminate container (See Picture 2)
3
Park Flexi Truck to the left or right so that they are staggered, and you can walk between Flexi Trucks.
4
Position the Flexi, apply the handbrake.
5
To remove from the container is the reverse of the above.
NOTE: The Flexi is a pivot steer truck and therefore has different manoeuvring characteristics to a conventional counter balance FLT.
