📝 Sequence of Work
Removal and Refitting of Forks on a Fork Carriage
1
Refer to SWP 001 Before Starting Work/ Putting Forklift Truck in Safe Position before carrying out the following work.
2
Raise the forks just clear of the floor.
3
Remove the appropriate fork retaining setscrews from the carriage.
4
Slide each fork to the appropriate position and remove each fork with the fork handling trolley, Picture 1.
5
Refitting the forks is the reversal of the above.
6
Note: Over a period of time forks get worn and thickness reduces. The main area of wear is the heel. The amount of wear can be determined using calipers for measurement. If the percentage of wear is in excess of 10%, the forks must be replaced. Put any such forks (with percentage of wear of over 10 % at the heel) in the scrap bin and replace with new ones. A lot of the original strength has been lost and they should not continue to be used.
7
Note: Only a manufacturer or fork specialist can modify or repair forks.
